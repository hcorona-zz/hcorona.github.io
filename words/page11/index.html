On what comes after us

30 Jul 2014

Pictures of you are still hanging on the wall.
I am not ready to change from lovers to whatever is next
Sometimes I miss you and sometimes I don’t, all in the same day. 

 I don’t know what to say,
neither how to shut up
and i definitely don’t want to hurt you.
